Extension v1.18.0 has been released.

<!-- truncate -->

- [@ShanaryS](https://github.com/ShanaryS) made huge enhancements and fixes to the statistics and annotations features. WaniKani integration, more advanced projected stats, and more.
- [@jprostko](https://github.com/jprostko) fixed subtitle detection on Hulu and made general improvements to subtitle detection code. Another huge thanks for their high quality contributions.

Shout out to first-time contributors:

- [@nerisal](https://github.com/nerisal) localized asbplayer in Italian. Thank you for taking the time to do translate the 100s of strings in asbplayer.
- [@Roka20012](https://github.com/Roka20012) added a way to download audio from the Anki export dialog.
- [@Dr-TNineS](https://github.com/Dr-TNineS) made it possible to switch between anime and drama categories for Jimaku subtitle downloads.

Read the full release notes for v1.18.0 [here](https://github.com/asbplayer/asbplayer/releases/tag/v1.18.0).
